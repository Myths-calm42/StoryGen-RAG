Ravi had lived in the same village his whole life, but tonight, the old well behind his house was glowing blue. He stepped closer, his heart pounding, and peered over the edge. A faint, ancient voice called his name from somewhere deep below.

He should have run. Instead, he lowered the old rope ladder his grandfather kept coiled by the barn, and began to climb down into the dark.

At the bottom, he found not water, but a small chamber lit by strange glowing stones embedded in the walls. In the center stood a woman in robes made of starlight, her eyes the same blue as the well's glow.

"You have finally come," she said. "Your grandfather promised you would, before he passed. There is something he left for you here."

Ravi's breath caught. His grandfather had died three years ago, and had never once mentioned a hidden chamber beneath their well.
